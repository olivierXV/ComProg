My Insights in the BSKE 2023

The Barangay and SK Elections being held this year will be essential not only because it will finally be held after 5 years. It will also show the sense of duty of us Filipinos in how we vote in the elections. Our choices will represent how we choose our leaders and future leaders, how we as citizens choose our future.
