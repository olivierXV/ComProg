Websites and their History

Facebook

Facebook is a social media site that has messaging in it. According to the web search results, the original purpose of Facebook was to allow Harvard students to use their “.edu” email addresses and photos to connect with other students at the school

Google

Google is the biggest search engine and email providers, it's mission is to organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and useful.

Yahoo

Yahoo is now one of the largest search engines and mail provider, it was created was to serve as a guide to the World Wide Web, a directory of other websites organized in a hierarchy
